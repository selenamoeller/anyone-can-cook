Dish: Shakshuka

Shakshuka is a vegetarian spiced dish of saucy tomatoes, peppers, and onions with cracked eggs lightly poached into the sauce.

Country of Origin: Tunisia

Shakshuka is said to have orgiated in Tunisia, but it is widely popular across most of Northern Africa.

Ingredients:

  • Olive Oil
  • 1 Chopped Yellow Onion
  • 2 Chopped Green Peppers
  • 2 Minced Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Tsp Ground Coriander
  • 1 Tsp Paprika
  • .5 Tsp Ground Cumin
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 6 Chopped Tomatoes
  • .5 Cup Tomato Sauce
  • 6 Eggs
  • .25 Cup Chopped Parkley Leaves
  • .25 Cup Chopped Mint Leaves

Directions:

  1. Heat oil in large cast iron skillet. Add in onions, green peppers, garlic, spices, and salt and pepper to taste. Cook on medium-high heat for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionaly.
  2. Add tomatoes and sauce. Cove and simmer for 15 minutes on medium heat. Uncover and cook for 5 more minutes.
  3. Using a spoon, made 6 indentations and crack an egg into each.
  4. Cover and cook on low for about 5 minutes, or until eggs are cooked to desired doneness.
  5. Serve sprinkled with parsley and mint.

Servings: 6 People

Prep Time: 10 Minutes

Cook Time: 30 Minutes
